FastPlay is a fast registration solution allowing Canadian consumers to instantly start playing.

With FastPlay, the end-user’s method of payment will identify the end-user while also providing and clarifying their personal details which are then made available to and stored by the operator.

This feature will provide more security as players will not be required to verify their identity or provide details manually.
