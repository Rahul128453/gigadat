INSIGHTS

General market insights

Canada is ethnically a diverse country with includes as native languages Mandarin, Cantonese, Punjabi, Spanish, Tagalog and Arabic in addition to French and English.

Based on these statistics, all Canadian Banks, Trust and Insurance Companies have customer support in both official languages - English, and French, as well as in Chinese and Spanish due to the growing prevalence of these ethnic populations in Canada.

BANKING

Canada’s online and mobile banking key figures

There are nearly 300 financial institutions in Canada which include banks, insurance companies providing banking services and small credit unions.

However, the market is largely dominated by the “big six”, which are Canada’s biggest financial institutions: Royal Bank of Canada (RBC), Bank of Montreal (BMO), Trust Dominion Bank (TD Bank), National Bank of Canada, the Bank of Nova Scotia (Scotia Bank) and finally Canadian Imperial Bank of Commerce (CIBC).
